Oh, the Fun of coding,
when all the tags align, when every line has things like br or p where theres no errors to see,
just pure coding

Why i see a red x,
how come i believe my work is right,
how come i cant understand the language,
Something isnt right Whyy!,

I believe i can do it,
but what if i cant

Is the Sky Bright,

Or is the Sky White
who knows what i see in sight ...
